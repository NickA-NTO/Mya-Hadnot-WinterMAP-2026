Root Causes Identified:
  • Amplify Phase-Out Without Replacement (Nov 21): Amplify was delivering 28-63 XP/day at 85% accuracy through Nov 21, then stopped. No comparable reading app was rostered to replace it. This created a ~30 min/day gap in structured reading instruction during the critical pre-MAP window.
  • Lexia Core5 Placement Mismatch: Student shows 40-70% accuracy in Lexia (Oct-Nov), well below the 80% target indicating content too hard. On Oct 16, accuracy was just 40%. This app consumed 30-70 min/day but produced minimal XP (0.1-0.3 XP/Min) due to misplacement.
  • ClearFluency G1 Comprehension Struggles: Despite strong fluency (80-126 WCPM on G1 texts, far exceeding 67 target), quiz accuracy was inconsistent: 40-80% range. Student is a "word caller" - decodes well but struggles with comprehension at higher ATOS levels (3.0-4.0+ stories assigned).
  • Late G2 ClearFluency Start: Student passed G1 ORF benchmark (67 WCPM @ 95%) but wasn't moved to G2 until Dec 27 - only 5 weeks before Winter MAP. Earlier advancement would have provided more G2-level comprehension practice.
Remediation Plan
  1. 1. Reallocate Lexia Time to ClearFluency G2
    Lexia is consuming 30-70 min/day at 40-70% accuracy with minimal XP production. Reduce Lexia time and reallocate to ClearFluency G2 where student shows stronger engagement. Monitor Lexia accuracy - if it doesn't improve to 80%+, consider full phase-out.
  2. 2. Add Comprehension-Focused Intervention
    Student's fluency (80+ WCPM) exceeds grade benchmarks but comprehension lags (60-75% quiz scores). Add targeted comprehension practice - consider MobyMax Reading or similar app focused on main idea, supporting details, and inference skills flagged by MAP.
  3. 3. Adjust ClearFluency G2 ATOS Levels
    Recent G2 stories (ATOS 3.5-5.2) may be too challenging for comprehension despite adequate fluency. Request ATOS 2.0-3.0 range stories to build comprehension confidence before advancing to harder texts.
  4. 4. Continue Lalilo G2 (Effective)
    Lalilo shows strong metrics: 90%+ accuracy, 1.2 XP/Min, G2 test-outs passed (85%, 80%, 85%). Lalilo is addressing Language skills effectively. Maintain current allocation.
